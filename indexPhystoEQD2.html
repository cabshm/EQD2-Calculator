EQD2 Calculator from Physical Dose

Quick physical dose → BED and EQD2 calculator using the linear-quadratic model.

Inputs

Total prescribed or delivered physical dose.
Must be a positive whole number.
Common examples: tumor 10 Gy, late-responding tissue 3 Gy.
For educational and calculation support only. Verify all clinical calculations independently before patient care use.

Results

Dose per fraction 2.00 Gy/fx
BED 72.00 Gy
EQD2 60.00 Gy

Formula

d = D / n
BED = D × (1 + d / α/β)
EQD2 = BED / (1 + 2 / α/β)

Equivalent:
EQD2 = D × (d + α/β) / (2 + α/β)
